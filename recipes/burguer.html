Burguer Recipe

Description

This is an easy and delicious burguer recipe. Please follow the instrucions!

Ingredients:

  • Ground Beef
  • Burguer Buns
  • Onion
  • Cheese
  • Bacon Bites
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Mustard and Ketchup
  • Cheeses

Steps:

  1. Mix the ground beef with salt, pepper, garlic powder and form the paddy
  2. Cook the onion in butter and then add the cheese and set aside
  3. Cook the paddy medium rare
  4. Assemble the burguer by putting a little mustard and ketchup in the buns, the paddy, the melted cheese with the onions
  5. Scramble the eggs with salt and pepperd
  6. Top it over with the eggs and bacon bites and enjoy
